Our Mission

We believe every child with a speech impairment deserves engaging, effective practice — not just in the clinic, but at home, every single day.

Why we built SpeechBuddy

Speech therapy is most effective when children practise consistently — but traditional methods make home practice difficult to track, manage, and motivate.

SpeechBuddy bridges the gap between the clinic and home. Therapists assign exercises. Kids practise through a fun, game-like app. Progress flows back automatically, so therapists always know exactly how their patients are doing.

We're a team of Software Engineering students at the University of Copenhagen working on this as our Bachelor Thesis, guided by real feedback from speech-language pathologists.
