\section{Problem Statement}
\label{sec:problem-statement}

Children with speech impairments often require frequent and consistent practice to improve their speech abilities, but traditional therapy sessions provide limited time for repetition and guidance. This creates a need for a digital tool that can support structured, engaging, and accessible speech practice outside clinical settings.

In this chapter, introduce and justify the selected problem. Explain who is affected by the problem, why it is relevant, and why it is suitable for investigation in a software engineering project.

Suggested points to include:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item Why consistent practice matters for children with speech impairments.
  \item Why therapy sessions alone may not provide enough repetition.
  \item How children, parents, caregivers, and therapists are affected.
  \item The cost of not addressing the problem, such as inconsistent practice, low motivation, or limited progress visibility.
  \item The software engineering opportunity: building a structured, reliable, and usable support tool.
\end{itemize}
