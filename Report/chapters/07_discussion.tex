\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

This chapter should reflect on the project journey and discuss the effectiveness of the solution. It should connect the technical work back to the original problem and consider the limitations of the outcome.

Suggested points:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
  \item How effectively the solution addressed the selected problem.
  \item What worked well in the application and development process.
  \item What technical or design challenges were encountered.
  \item Limitations of the implementation and evaluation.
  \item Ethical considerations, especially because the intended users are children.
  \item Privacy considerations related to audio, speech, or user data.
  \item Lessons learned about software engineering practice.
  \item Improvements that could be made in future work.
\end{itemize}
